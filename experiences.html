My experiences

UCandela

Together with my friend, we started Ucandela, a tuition platform aimed at connecting tutors and tutees.

We hire Junior College tutors because they can relate to the tutees better and know the syllabus very well.

Project Vitamindz

Together with my friends, we started Project Vitamindz, a project dedicated to giving underprivileged kids free enrichment lessons.

Sessions are conducted weekly for 2 hours and include different experiments like Slime making and Bottled Terrarium.
